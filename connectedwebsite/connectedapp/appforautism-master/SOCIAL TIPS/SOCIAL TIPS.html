SOCIALIZE WITH ME

Tip#1: Always say PLEASE and THANK YOU when someone has helped you.

Tip#2:When you want to approach someone, don't be too agressive. Be calm, and start the conversation by saying "Hello" with a smile (NOT A SCARY ONE).

Tip#3:If you want to talk to a person who is currently talking to someone else, wait until their conversation is over.



Tip#4:If you need to give someone very important information that cannot wait, say "Excuse me, can I talk to you for a second?"

Tip#5: When having a conversation make sure there is distance between you and the person you are speaking to. Don't stand too close or too far.

TIP#6:While in a conversation, try to identify the person's emotions while they talk. (If you need help with identifying emotions look at the EMOJI ME icon)



Tip#7: Always try to be respectful while having a conversation.

Tip#8: Show people that you care about what they are saying by making eye contact. If this is too hard, try looking at their nose or forehead (They won't know the difference!)

Tip#9: Sometimes saying the truth can hurt someone's feelings.



Tip#10: If you would like to get yourself out of the conversation try saying "Do you know what time it is? I think I have to go" or "I have to go. It was really nice talking to you."

Tip#11: Don't be scared to interact with others. (Have Confidence)

Tip#12: Even if you don't chose to socialize, try to build up these skills just in case.
